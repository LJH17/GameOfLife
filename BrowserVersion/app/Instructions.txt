The Game of Life
----------------

To Play:

1. Ensure http-server is running, achieved by running "npm start" from the terminal at the root directory.
2. Open "GameOfLife.html" from the index list or navigate to "http://localhost:7000/GameOfLife.html".

Operation:

The game first randomly creates an initial grid of cells.
The game then runs indefinitely, applying the requested rules.
The canvas HTML element graphically illustrates the cells.
